Basic Short Notes & Questions for Chapters 1–4

Chapter 1: Atomic Structure

  • Atom is the smallest unit of matter.
  • Composed of nucleus (protons + neutrons) and electrons.
  • Atomic number = number of protons.
  • Mass number = protons + neutrons.
  • Electrons occupy shells (K, L, M…) following 2n² rule.

  10. Who proposed the planetary model of atom?

Chapter 2: Periodic Table

  • Elements arranged in increasing atomic number.
  • Groups → vertical columns, similar chemical properties.
  • Periods → horizontal rows, properties change gradually.
  • Metals on left, non-metals on right.
  • Periodic trends: Atomic size decreases across period, increases down group.

  9. What is the most reactive non-metal?
  10. Give an example of halogen.

Chapter 3: Chemical Bonding

  • Bond → force holding atoms together.
  • Types: Ionic, Covalent, Metallic.
  • Ionic → transfer of electrons, forms ions.
  • Covalent → sharing of electrons.
  • Metallic → delocalized electrons in metal lattice.

  4. How is a covalent bond formed?

  6. Give example of covalent compound.

  8. What is a polar covalent bond?

  10. What is an electron dot structure?

Chapter 4: Organic Chemistry Basics

  • Organic compounds → contain mainly carbon and hydrogen.
  • Hydrocarbons → compounds made of carbon and hydrogen only.
  • Alkanes → single bonds, saturated.
  • Alkenes → double bonds, unsaturated.
  • Alkynes → triple bonds, unsaturated.
  • Functional groups → OH, COOH, NH2 etc. define chemical properties.
