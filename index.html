âœ… Chapter 1: Matter in Our Surroundings

  • Matter: Jo space occupy kare aur mass rakhe.
  • 3 States: Solid, Liquid, Gas.
  • Solid: fixed shape & volume.
  • Liquid: fixed volume but no fixed shape.
  • Gas: no fixed shape & no fixed volume.
  • Diffusion: particles ka mix hona.
  • Evaporation cooling effect deta hai.
  • Temperature badhne se particles fast move karte hain.

âœ… Chapter 2: Is Matter Around Us Pure?

  • Pure substance: single type of particles.
  • Mixture: two ya more substances mixed.
  • Homogeneous: mixture uniform hota hai (salt water).
  • Heterogeneous: mixture uniform nahi hota (sand + water).
  • Solution = Solute + Solvent
  • Colloid: milk, fog.
  • Suspension: muddy water.
  • Separation methods: filtration, evaporation, distillation.

  • Law of Conservation of Mass: mass kabhi destroy nahi hota.
  • Atomic mass: atom ka mass.
  • Molecular mass: molecule ka total mass.

  • Atom me nucleus hota hai.
  • Nucleus me proton (+) aur neutron (0).
